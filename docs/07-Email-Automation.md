# Email Automation

## Objective

Automatically send a confirmation email to every HOT lead.

## Trigger

HOT classification.

## Workflow

```text
HOT
 │
 ▼
SMTP
 │
 ▼
Email
```

## Email Content

- Greeting
- Confirmation
- Follow-up notification
- Closing

## Result

Every HOT lead receives an automatic confirmation email immediately after qualification.
