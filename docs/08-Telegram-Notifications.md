# Telegram Notifications

## Objective

Notify the sales team immediately when a HOT lead is detected.

## Trigger

HOT classification.

## Workflow

```text
HOT Lead
    │
    ▼
Telegram Bot
    │
    ▼
Sales Team
```

## Notification Content

- Lead name
- Company
- Position
- Email
- Budget
- AI score
- Classification
- AI reason
- AI summary

## Result

Every HOT lead generates an instant Telegram notification, allowing the sales team to react quickly.
